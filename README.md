# IOI in GPT-2 small — attribution patching of every hidden coordinate

One self-contained notebook (`ioi_full_activations.ipynb`) that applies
**attribution patching with [nnsight](https://nnsight.net)** to **every hidden
coordinate** of GPT-2 small on the **IOI** task — components, attention-head
dimensions, and MLP neurons — then:

1. **recovers** the IOI circuit of Wang et al. (2022),
2. **traces** the paper's circuit (Fig 2) annotated with the new numbers,
3. **regenerates the paper's figures** (Figs 1, 2, 3b, 3c, 4b, 5b, **6, 7, 8**) and
   the MLP appendix (**Fig 19 / Appendix J**),
4. goes **one level below components** — to neurons in the MLPs and dimensions
   inside attention heads — and redoes Figs 6 & 7 in the **neuron basis**,
5. **stores** the full coordinate-level attributions in a **Zarr** store (the
   "storage challenge").

Dataset: [`mib-bench/ioi`](https://huggingface.co/datasets/mib-bench/ioi).
Paper: *Interpretability in the Wild* (arXiv:2211.00593), in `paper/`.
Neuron-basis framing follows [transluce.org/neuron-circuits](https://transluce.org/neuron-circuits).

## Method

Denoising attribution patching with nnsight, metric = `logit(IO) − logit(S)`:
`attr = (a_clean − a_corrupt) · ∂metric_corrupt/∂a_corrupt`, computed for every
hidden unit from one clean forward + one corrupt forward + one backward.
Gradients use nnsight's `retain_grad()` + `.grad.save()` inside
`with metric.backward():`. Intermediate-node figures (4b/5b) use **edge
attribution patching**; the completeness/minimality/MLP figures (6/7/19) use
**mean-ablation knockouts** (the paper's p_ABC mean).

## Key results (GPT-2 small, ~500 IOI examples)

- Clean logit diff ≈ **+3.4** (paper 3.56); ~**19–20/26** circuit heads recovered in the top-26.
- **Fig 3b** name movers 9.9/9.6/10.0 (+), negative name movers 10.7/11.10 (−);
  **Fig 4b** lights up S-inhibition 7.3/7.9/8.6/8.10; **Fig 5b** lights up induction 5.5/6.9.
- **Fig 7 (minimality)** matches the paper's per-class pattern; **Fig 6 (completeness)**
  places the 26-head circuit near `y=x`.
- **Fig 19**: knocking out **MLP0 reverses the sign of the logit difference**; all other
  MLP layers barely matter (reproduces Appendix J).
- **Neuron basis:** the MLP contribution is **sparse** (a few hundred MLP neurons recover
  the full-model logit diff) while attention is **dense** in the coordinate basis (heads
  need their whole 64-dim output). Beyond the paper, head **9.4** is a robust non-canonical
  (negative-name-mover-like) head.
